Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Short version: OnlyDraws collects nothing. There is no account, no sign-in, and no server. Everything you draw and every score you earn stays on your device.

Who we are

OnlyDraws (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a drawing practice game published by KoalaLoop Games on the Apple App Store. This policy explains how the app handles information. If you have questions, contact us at support@koalaloop.games.

Information we collect

None that leaves your device. OnlyDraws has no backend and makes no network requests to us. All of your activity, including:

  • your drawings and stroke data,
  • scores, mastery, streaks, achievements, and daily results,
  • and your app settings (such as the chosen theme),

is stored only in local storage on your device. We cannot see it, and it is never transmitted, sold, or shared.

Purchases

OnlyDraws offers a single one-time in-app purchase to unlock the full game. Payments are processed entirely by Apple. We never receive or store your payment details, your Apple Account, or your name. Apple’s handling of purchase information is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. To restore a previous purchase, use Settings › Restore Purchase in the app; this asks Apple to confirm your unlock and involves no personal data on our side.

Diagnostics you choose to share with Apple

If you have turned on “Share With App Developers” in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Analytics & Improvements, Apple may provide us with aggregated, anonymized crash and usage reports through App Store Connect. These reports do not identify you, and you control this setting in iOS at any time.

Children’s privacy

OnlyDraws does not collect data from anyone, including children. Because nothing is collected or transmitted, no personal information about a child (or any user) is ever gathered.

Your control and data deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in control of it. To erase your scores, mastery, streaks, and achievements, open Settings › Reset all data in the app. Deleting the app from your device also removes all associated local data.

Third parties

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only third party involved is Apple, as the platform and payment processor.

Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles information, we will update this page and revise the date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@koalaloop.games.
